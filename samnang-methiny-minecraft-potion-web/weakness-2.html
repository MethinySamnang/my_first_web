Potion of Weakness II

Description

The Potion of Weakness II is a more potent version of the standard Potion of Weakness. It is created by adding Glowstone Dust to a standard Potion of Weakness using a Brewing Stand. Potion of Weakness II reduces the damage dealt by melee attacks to a greater extent than the standard version. It can be useful in specific situations where you need to weaken a target even further.

Ingredients

To make Potion of Weakness II, You will need:

  • Water Bottle

    You will need empty glass bottles, which you can obtain by right-clicking on a water source or by using glass bottles in your crafting grid.

  • Nether Wart

    Nether Wart is a plant that grows in the Nether. You'll need at least one Nether Wart for the brewing process.

  • Fermented Spider Eye

    This is the main ingredient to transform an Awkward Potion into a Potion of Weakness.

  • Glowstone Dust

    this will turn it into Potion Weakness II

  • Blaze Powder

    Blaze Powder is used as fuel for the Brewing Stand.

  • Brewing Stand

    You'll need a Brewing Stand to create and modify potions.

Steps

Follow the Following Steps to Make Potion of Weakness II:

  • Create Awkward Potion

    -Start by placing Blaze Powder in the fuel slot of a Brewing Stand to activate it.
    -Add Water Bottles to the three bottle slots in the Brewing Stand to create Awkward Potions.
    -Place Nether Wart in the top slot of the Brewing Stand to create an Awkward Potion.

  • Add Fermented Spider Eye

    Put a Fermented Spider Eye in the top slot. This will create a Potion of Weakness (Standard).

  • Add Glowstone Dust

    Put Glowstone Dust in the top slot. This will create a Potion of Weakness II.
