Potion of Leaping

Description

Leaping Potion, also known as a Potion of Leaping, is a type of potion that, when consumed, grants the player the ability to jump higher for a limited duration. The Potion of Leaping can be useful for overcoming obstacles, reaching higher places, or simply enhancing your mobility in the game.

Ingredients

To make Extended Potion of Leaping, You will need:

  • Water Bottle

    You will need empty glass bottles, which you can obtain by right-clicking on a water source or by using glass bottles in your crafting grid.

  • Nether Wart

    Nether Wart is a plant that grows in the Nether. You'll need at least one Nether Wart for the brewing process.

  • Rabbit's Foot

    Rabbit's Foot is the key ingredient for creating a Potion of Leaping. You can obtain Rabbit's Foot as a rare drop from killing rabbits.

  • Blaze Powder

    Blaze Powder is used as fuel for the Brewing Stand.

  • Redstone Dust

    This is used to extend the duration of your potion.

  • Brewing Stand

    You'll need a Brewing Stand to create and modify potions.

Steps

Follow the Following Steps to Make Extended Potion of Leaping:

  • Prepare Water Bottle

    Place Water Bottles in the Brewing Stand and add Blaze Powder as fuel. This will create three awkward potions.

  • Add Awkward Potion

    An Awkward Potion is a base potion in Minecraft.

  • Add Nether Wart

    Place Nether Wart in the Brewing Stand's ingredient slot to convert the Water Bottles into Potion of Awkward Leaping.

  • Add Rabbit's Foot

    Add the Rabbit's Foot to the Brewing Stand to create the Potion of Leaping.

  • Add Redstone Dust

    Add Redstone Dust to the Brewing Stand to extend the duration of the potion, turning it into an Extended Potion of Leaping.
