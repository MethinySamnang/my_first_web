Potion of Slowness

Description

Potion of Slowness is a type of potion that, when consumed or thrown as a Splash Potion, will slow down the movement speed of the player or entity it affects. This potion is often used in various in-game scenarios, such as in combat or in certain redstone contraptions, to hinder the movement of mobs or other players.

Ingredients

To make Extended Potion of Slowness, You will need:

  • Water Bottle

    You will need empty glass bottles, which you can obtain by right-clicking on a water source or by using glass bottles in your crafting grid.

  • Fermented Spider Eye

    Sugar is used to create a Potion of Swiftness.

  • Nether Wart

    Nether Wart is a plant that can be found in the Nether dimension. You'll need to gather Nether Wart, which can be farmed in the Overworld with soul sand, or you can find it in Nether Fortresses in the Nether dimension.

  • Blaze Powder

    Blaze Powder is used as a fuel for the brewing stand. You'll need one Blaze Powder for every brewing session. You can obtain Blaze Powder by crafting Blaze Rods in your crafting table.

  • Brewing Stand

    You'll need a Brewing Stand to create and modify potions.

Steps

Follow the Following Steps to Make Extended Potion of Slowness:

  • Obtain Blaze Powder

    Travel to the Nether and defeat Blazes to obtain Blaze Rods.
    Craft Blaze Rods into Blaze Powder by placing them in the crafting grid.

  • Create Water Bottle

    To make water bottles, right-click on a water source (like a river or lake) with your empty glass bottles.

  • Create potion of Slowness

    Place the Blaze Powder in one of the brewing stand's fuel slots.
    Put the Water Bottles in the brewing stand, and they will turn into Awkward Potions.
    Add Nether Wart to the brewing stand to create a base Potion.

  • Modify with Fermented Spider Eye

    Once you have the base Potion, add a Fermented Spider Eye to the brewing stand. This will create a Potion of Slowness.

  • Modify with Redstone

    To make an Extended Potion of Slowness, you will need to add Redstone Dust to the brewing stand after creating the standard Potion of Slowness. This will extend the potion's duration.
